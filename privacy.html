Privacy Policy

Story Spindle Daily

1. Introduction

Story Spindle Daily ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Data We Collect

When you authorize Story Spindle Daily to access your TikTok account, we receive the following information from TikTok:

TikTok Account Information:
  • Your TikTok username
  • Your TikTok profile picture URL
  • Your TikTok user ID
  • Your authorization token to post content on your behalf

We do NOT collect your TikTok password, email address, location data, or browsing history.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively to publish videos to your TikTok account through TikTok's official API, as authorized by you. We do NOT use your data for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing

Story Spindle Daily does NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is transmitted to TikTok only to post content on your behalf through TikTok's official API.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only while you actively use the App. Upon request or if you revoke authorization, we permanently delete all stored data within 30 days. Contact taganfarrell14@gmail.com for data deletion requests.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, delete, or revoke authorization for your data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact taganfarrell14@gmail.com.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at taganfarrell14@gmail.com.
